Visit Caserta!

Discover the Reggia di Caserta: Italy's spectacular royal palace often called the 'Italian Versailles'.

What to do in Caserta:

Visit the 'Reggia di Caserta':

This UNESCO World Heritage site is one of the largest royal residences in the world, featuring stunning Baroque architecture, grand staircases, and beautifully landscaped gardens with fountains and waterfalls.
Click here to visit the official website of the Reggia di Caserta.

Visit the The Royal Park

Explore the vast Royal Park at Reggia di Caserta, a stunning Baroque landscape by Vanvitelli. Marvel at its grand central waterway with monumental fountains, a UNESCO World Heritage highlight.
Click here to learn more about the Royal Park.

Eat some good pizza!

Savor the pizza of Caserta, celebrated for its exceptional dough and premium local ingredients. Experience a modern take on Neapolitan tradition, famed for top-quality buffalo mozzarella and a light, digestible crust, earning it international acclaim.
